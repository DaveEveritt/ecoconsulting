Research and papers

This section contains papers and other research undertaken with various partners for arts projects, academic establishments and conferences; concentrating on emerging technology, programming culture, creativity and complexity, disability and technology, and the cultural impact and uptake of computing culture.

Most of the published work is trans- or multi-disciplinary, and covers complexity, art-science-technology, human-computer interfaces, creativity and social issues.

Please respect ownership and take care to reference the work if quoted elsewhere.
