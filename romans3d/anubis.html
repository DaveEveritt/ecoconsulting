  • Findspot: Vine Street
  • Location: 52.6387, -1.1387

Roman carved ivory box inlay depicting the Egyptian god Anubis.

An extremely rare find in the Western Roman Empire, made more unusual as it depicts the Egyptian god Anubis. Anubis is shown above a lotus flower as he holds two lances. This box inlay is made of ivory and would have been a very high status object, even in Egypt.
